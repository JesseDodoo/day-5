What is a metonic series?

A metonic series or cycle is a period of 19 calendar years (235 lunar months), after which the new and full moons return to the same or nearly same dates of the year. There are only 235 full moons yet only 228 calendar months in the 19 year metonic cycle. Because there are more full moons than calendar months, at least 7 of the calendar months will have 2 full moons.
